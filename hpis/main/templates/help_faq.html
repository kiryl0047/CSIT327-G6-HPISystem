Help Center & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about appointments, services, and policies.

Appointment Scheduling

How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by filling out the **Patient Information Form** located at the bottom of our Home page. Our administrative staff will review your request and assign a doctor.
What happens after I submit the request?
After submission, you will receive an email confirmation that your request has been logged. Our team processes requests within 24 hours and will email you the confirmed date, time, and attending physician details.

Health Records & Privacy

Is my patient information kept private?
Yes. We adhere strictly to national health privacy standards. All patient data is encrypted, secured, and only accessible by authorized healthcare staff involved in your care.
How can I get a copy of my records?
Please contact our administrative office directly via phone or the Contact Us link in the footer. We require photo identification to process any request for medical records.
